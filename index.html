Breaking News

The National Bank Of Canada is suing Amanda Lang because of her tip on how every Canadian person can become really rich.

In a live broadcast, the journalist regretted telling the truth. However, it was already too late.

By Raisa Patel Ottawa Bureau
Thursday, December 21, 2023 6 min to read
Article was updated Dec 21, 2023
JOIN THE CONVERSATION

«I'm afraid of poverty and I can't understand poor people,» said Amanda Lang on the live broadcast. ”I use every opportunity to become wealthier and I know a way that can make any Canadian citizen a millionaire. That's my personal secret».

REGISTER

The scandal flared up in the middle of a live broadcast of «The Exchange» when Amanda Lang accidentally revealed a link on her cell phone. The attentive viewers immediately grasped the link, whereupon The National Bank Of Canada called the TV studio and asked for the live broadcast to be interrupted.

However, we managed to persuade the editor of «The Exchange» to provide us with a copy of the controversial program. Please note, however, that this item could be removed at any time. So if you are lucky enough to be able to read this article, sign up using this link that Amanda Lang has kept secret for so long.

DELETED INTERVIEW WITH AMANDA LANG THAT ALL BANKS FEAR.

Amanda Lang: «I'll tell you something now: you don't have to work to be wealthy. Once you understand that, you won't have to worry about money anymore».

Dianne Buckner: «If there really is a possibility, why not share it?»

Amanda Lang: «Why should I reveal my secrets if I don't get anything in return? Don't even try to figure out how I've made money the last three years. I won't show anyone, even for a lot of money. Otherwise people will never learn to look for new opportunities. Also, if it comes out how I'm doing it, it could hurt the economy nationwide».

Dianne Buckner: «To what extent could knowing how to make money hurt Canadian people?»

Amanda Lang: «Just think about it. If someone knows they can become a millionaire in two months, why is that person still working? Of course, my strategy is available to everyone, but should everyone become wealthy, there will be no one left to work in shops, catering and manufacturing».

Dianne Buckner: «That sounds too good to be true. I always thought you were an honest person, but I don't really believe you at this point».

Amanda Lang: «Should I lie? Good. I will show everyone that any poor person in Canada can earn thousands of CAD a day in just five minutes. And I'll show you how much I make using this method. I don't want to go into details, just want to say that it has to do with stocks: stocks make everyone rich these days».

Amanda Lang opened something on her cell phone and showed it to the moderator. The moderator was amazed. Amanda Lang: «Well, what do you think of it? Isn't that great? And guess how much money I put into it? Only 350 CAD. And now this program saves me tens of thousands a day and runs even when I'm sleeping». After this sentence, a cameraman ran to the moderator and whispered something in her ear.

Dianne Buckner: «I was just told that when you handed me your mobile phone, the camera caught the link to the program. All viewers of the show have now noticed that».

REGISTER

Amanda Lang was then appalled. What she had been dealing with for so long was published in a live broadcast.

Amanda Lang: «One call from me is enough and this program will never be broadcast. I request that you cut out the link that was shown. I don't want to share it with anyone».

Dianne Buckner: «You know that we're live, right? You don't need to hide anything anymore. It would be much more helpful if you informed Canadian people about this earning opportunity».

Amanda Lang: «I've been using the platform for three years. All you would have to do is follow this link and then log in to the platform. You would have to deposit at least 350 CAD. This is the minimum amount required for the program to work well. After that, you don't have to do anything else: the platform works by itself. All you have to do is check your account balance regularly and transfer your profit».

Dianne Buckner: «What does it take to understand this program and earn as much as you with it?»

Amanda Lang: «No skills or special knowledge are required at all. I can prove it. Please give me your cell phone. I'll sign you up to TokenTact and you'll see for yourself how much you've made towards the end of the interview».

Dianne Buckner handed her mobile phone to Amanda Lang to register. The registration took place within a few minutes and the minimum amount was paid.

Dianne Buckner: «And that's all? What do you have to do now?»

Amanda Lang: «Nothing at all. In about 20 minutes we will see how much money is in the account».

Dianne Buckner: «If the program really works, what can the Canadian do who cannot even raise the minimum amount?»

Amanda Lang: «When I found out about TokenTact, I didn't have a lot of money at my disposal either, we were in the time of the pandemic. However, they can fund their account by credit card. What do you get out of money that doesn't work? It's an opportunity not to be missed».

After about 20 minutes it was time to check the balance.

Amanda Lang: «Now honestly, how much did you earn in 20 minutes?»

Dianne Buckner: «464 CAD - net profit, the account balance is now 814 CAD! I can't believe this is actually supposed to work».

The National Bank Of Canada then called and asked for the live broadcast to be stopped. If everyone knew about TokenTact, the banking system would lose customers. People would only use their bank cards to withdraw their winnings. That would be anything but profitable for the banks. After the live broadcast was interrupted, our editor decided to test this earning opportunity himself and wrote a report about it afterward.

Martin Walsh - News Editor

DAY 1:

«I have to admit that I didn't trust this platform at first, but I really wanted to try it out personally. At the time of my research, I didn't even have funds for the minimum deposit, which is why I had to use a credit card. I invested 350 CAD and watched what happened. Now imagine my surprise when nothing happened after the first deposit. I already thought I had been scammed. But after a few minutes, the algorithm began to work. I was happy about that, but noticed the following statistic: I lost 22 CAD on my first trade! In the first few minutes, large losses could already be determined. But the next deal and the four that followed brought me money and in a few minutes my balance went from 350 to 372 CAD».

DAY 2:

«The morning started with me checking my bank balance. There were already 671 CAD recorded! Just imagine: in one day, the balance has doubled. I was about to withdraw the profit but decided to wait another week».

However, when I logged back in, I saw the following picture: almost 85% of all trades made were profitable. The remaining 15% resulted in losses but turned positive again. My account balance was 4350 CAD. I withdraw 4000 CAD to give my wife a gift. The money arrived within an hour
